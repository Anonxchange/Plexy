# Pexly

**Pexly**digital marketplace that connects users to trade value using
cryptocurrency and digital gift cards in a non‑custodial way.

Pexly does not operate as a wallet, bank, or financial institution.
Users always remain in control of their funds through their own external wallets.

---

## 🔹 What You Can Do on Pexly

### 🔸 Gift Card Marketplace
- Buy digital gift cards using cryptocurrency
- Sell unused or unwanted gift cards for crypto
- Trade gift cards directly with other users

All gift card transactions are facilitated through marketplace logic and verified delivery processes.

---

### 🔸 Crypto Trading (Non‑Custodial)
- Trade cryptocurrency peer‑to‑peer with other users
- Transactions are executed directly from user wallets
- Pexly never holds private keys or user funds

All blockchain transactions require explicit user wallet approval.

---

### 🔸 Third‑Party Crypto Purchase Links
Pexly may display links or integrations to independent third‑party services
that allow users to acquire cryptocurrency.

Pexly:
- Does not process payments
- Does not control pricing
- Does not act as a broker or intermediary

Users interact with third‑party providers at their own discretion.

---

## 🔐 Non‑Custodial Architecture

- Users connect their own external wallets
- Pexly never generates or stores wallet seed phrases
- All transactions are authorized by users via their wallets
- Smart contracts (where applicable) enforce transaction rules automatically

Pexly functions purely as a software interface and coordination layer.

---

## ⚠️ Important Disclaimers

- Pexly does not provide financial, investment, or custodial services
- Pexly does not guarantee the value, availability, or validity of third‑party gift cards
- Users are responsible for verifying transaction details before confirmation
- Cryptocurrency and digital asset transactions involve risk
